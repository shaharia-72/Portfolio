About Me

Hi, I'm Shaharia Hossain Sajjad, Based in Bangladesh

Hello! I’m a recent Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) graduate with a passion for software development and machine learning. Currently, I’m working on my thesis in machine learning, which has deepened my understanding of this exciting field.
